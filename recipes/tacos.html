Tacos recipe

How to make delicious homemade tacos!

So, never made tacos before? Fear not, this nice and simple recipe is easy to follow along and will have you making delicious tacos in no time!

Ingredient list:

For the Beer Batter:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup of beer
  • 1 egg

For the White Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, minced
  • 1 teaspoon minced capers
  • 1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried dill weed

For the Fish Tacos

  • 1 quart frying oil
  • 1 pound cod fillets, cut into 2 to 3 ounce portions
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 (12 ounce) package corn tortillas
  • 1/2 medium head cabbage, finely shredded

Great! We're all set! Now to the cooking:

  1. First lets make the beer batter! Combine flour, cornstarch, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl. Blend beer and egg in a different bowl then stir it into the flour mixture until only a few lumps remain.
  2. Second, make the white sauce. Mix together yogurt and mayonnaise in a bowl and gradually stir in lime juice until the consistency is slightly runny. Then season with jalapeno, capers, cayenne, oregano, cumin, and dill.
  3. Now heat the frying oil in a deep-fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  4. Dust the fish pieces with flour.
  5. Dip the floured fish into the beer batter and set aside.
  6. Fry the fish in the oil until crisp and golden brown. Drain on paper towels and lightly fry the tortillas until crisped but not too crisped! Then drain them on paper towels as well.
  7. Place fried fish onto the tortillas, top with shredded cabbage and white sauce. Enjoy!!!
