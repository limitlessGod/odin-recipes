Pizza recipe

Pizza Heaven!

Welcome to Pizza Heaven! This quick and easy recipe will have you making delicious homemade pizza in no time!
So what are you waiting for? Put on your apron and lets dive right in!

Ingredients

First off you'll need to get ingredients. The ingredients you will need are:

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons oliver oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Great! Now here is what you need to do to make your heavenly pizza:

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C) and lightly grease a pizza pan.
  2. Place warm water in a bowl and add in the yeast and sugar. Mix well for about 10 minutes until a creamy consistency is reached.
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture. Then beat until smooth.
  4. Let the mixture rest for 5 mintues.
  5. Lay the doughy mixture out onto a clean surface and pat/roll it into a 12-inch ish circle (you dont have to be perfect!)
  6. Great! Now its time to transfer our dough to the pizza pan. Now we'll spread our toppings and our sauce on our freshly made pizza crust!
  7. Bake in the oven for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown. Remove from oven and let sit for 5 minutes.
  8. Enjoy!!!
