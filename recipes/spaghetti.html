Spaghetti recipe

How to make the best Spaghetti ever!!!

Do you want to make the best spaghetti you've ever tasted? Better than restaurant quality?
Well, look no more for you have found the best spaghetti recipe to ever exist on the Internet.
This recipe will take you from A-Z walking you through every step to make spaghetti that you can finally be proud of!

Ingredients you will need:

  • 1 (8 ounce) package of Spaghetti
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1/2 cup butter, sliced-divided
  • 1 (8 ounce) container cottage cheese
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 (8 ounce) package shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

Steps to make the BEST spaghetti of your LIFE!!!

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Boil a large pot of salted water
  3. Cook spaghetti in the pot of boiling salt water for 8-12 minutes or until noodles are cooked through but firm to the bite. Then drain.
  4. While the pasta is cooking, heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir ground beef for 5-7 minutes or until browned and crumbly. Drain and dispose of grease.
  5. Transfer cooked ground beef to a bowl and add in the spaghetti sauce. Mix well.
  6. Place 1/2 butter slice into a casserole dish. Then spread out 1/2 of the spaghetti in the dish.
  7. Mix cottage cheese, cream cheese, and sour cream together in a bowl. Once thoroughly mixed, spread mixture over spaghetti in the casserole dish.
  8. Cover the mixture-covered spaghetti with the remaining 1/2 spaghetti and remaining butter.
  9. Pour ground beef mixture over the spaghetti and spread even.
  10. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes. Then spread Cheddar cheese on top and continue baking for 15 more minutes or until cheese is melted and browned.
  11. Enjoy!
