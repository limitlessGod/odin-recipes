Brownie recipe

THE BEST BROWNIES YOU HAVE EVER MADE!!!

Would you like to learn how to make the best brownies you have ever had, better than grandmas?
Well listen up and follow these instructions carefully because you are about to make AND taste the BEST brownes you have ever had!!!

Ingredient list

  • 1 1/2 cups unsalted butter
  • 3 cups white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 cups unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pound chopped walnuts

Quick and Easy steps to make the BEST Brownies EVER:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Get a baking pan, cover it with foil and cooking spray
    3. In a saucepan over medium-heat melt butter, stir in sugar until dissolved and then remove from heat. Gradually pour melted butter mixture into beaten eggs while whisking until fully mixed.
  3. Add in vanilla extract.
  4. Sift flour, cocoa, and salt together. Then add in the butter mixture and mix till blended and add in the walnuts.
  5. Spread the batter along the pan. Bake in the oven for 45-50 minutes or until the insides are crumbly and not gooey.
  6. Enjoy!!!
